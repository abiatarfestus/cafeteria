What it's about

This web application provides a platform for our customers to interact with us online. The platform is aimed to enhance convenience to our clients by allowing them to place orders as well as reserve seats in the cafeteria from the comfort of their office, home or elsewhere. This reduces frustration from queues while improving efficiency and time management.

Canteen Staff Registration

The canteen staff responsible for processing customer orders need to be registered and granted with Staff permission by the Admin. NB: Staff user accounts do not have permission to make seat reservations

Customer Registration

Only logged in customers can place orders and reserve seats online. A customer can create a personal user account by navigating to Register. Once an account is created, an activation request is sent to the Admin. The user can only login to their acount after it has been activated by the Admin. Once the account is activated, the user will be notified via email. Upon activation, the user can login to their account and update their profile with an image, a residential/delivery address and a cellphone number (to be used for SMS notification).

A logged in customer can reserve one of the open seats in the cafeteria. Once a reservation is made, it will be added to the list of active reservations, but with the Status of Pending. A notification is sent to staff emails to either accept or decline the reservation. (A confirmation of the reservation is also sent to the customer via email and SMS if there's a cellphone number on the customer's profile.) Once the reservation has been accepted/declined, another notification will be sent to the customer via email and SMS. Reservations will be updated to Expired status by the staff after it has been used. (An update is being developed to automate this process based on time intervals.) The customer can cancel their own reservation at any time from the Reservations list page.

Customers can place their orders online with or without delivery option. When an order with no delivery is done, the customer will be informed through the phone number on their profile. Customers can use any of the payment methods availabel at checkout page, which include the integrated PayPal or Debit/Credit card. Customer using non-integrated methods such as EFT and cellphone banking must include the payment reference number when submitting the order as no order will be prepared without confirmation that a payment was made.
